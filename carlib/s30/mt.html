5 Speed Conversion

-A 71B 5spd can be sourced from a Datsun 240K or 280ZX. Be sure to take note of the height of the clutch plate on the donor car. There are several lengths of clutch collars. A short collar can be corrected by using a longer slave pin or moving the slave closer using a custom adaptor plate. But using the correct length collar to begin with is easier.

Alternate slave cylinders

The slave cylinder from a S13 will fit the 71B bellhousing without issue.
